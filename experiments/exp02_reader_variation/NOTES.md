# exp02 — Reader cohort variation (Layer 2)

## Hypothesis

Higher-empathy and higher-transportability agents will report higher
emotional engagement and transportation on both texts. Trait → SWAS
correlations should be in expected directions and stable across seeds.

## Design

1. Sample a 50-agent cohort with `scripts/build_reader_cohort.py`.
2. Run the reading loop (`scripts/run_reading_session.py`) on both
   prototype texts.
3. Plot SWAS heatmaps (one per text) and a correlation matrix
   (traits × SWAS dimensions).
